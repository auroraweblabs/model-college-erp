Our History

  • 1989 Model College was established in M.I.D.C. Dombivli (East) in the year 1989. Since then, the college has grown phenomenally.
  • 2006 in the year 2006-2007, got “A grade” and the college was reaccredited with “A grade” in the year 2014-15 by NAAC. The College, a minority institution, also got U.G.C. recognition 2(f) 12 (B). In other words it means that the college is now permanently affiliated & recognized by the University Grant Commission, New Delhi. It is entitled to all the financial grants & other benefits.
  • 2009 College is also running centre for post graduate courses M.Com in Accountancy, Management and Banking and Finance from the year 2009.
